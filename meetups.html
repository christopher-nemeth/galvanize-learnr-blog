Meetup Information

React Denver @ Turing School of Software and Design. 2-20-18.

People I Met

  • Gabriel Konkel who is a front end developer who has a small consulting company with his brother who does the back end work.
  • Gabriel Camargo who works for Ingenious and manages developers. Currently working on ReasonML.

What I Learned

I learned that Konkel used GatsbyJS to work on a website for mikejohnsonforcolorado.com and some of the things GatsbyJS can do. While some of the things he said were over my head, one take away that I liked was that it allows your site to load its pages behind the scenes so it doesn't disrupt the user experience while making the site faster when navigating between pages. Additionally, I learned that Camargo is currently working on improving ReasonML which is new syntax for an old language called Ocaml AST. Again a lot of the information was over my head, but it was interesting how you can implement new syntax to old languages.

Takeaway Questions

  1. 1. Some of the take away questions I have were just more general questions regarding how JS,and these other frameworks talk with the back end and the benefits for implementing ReasonML into JS to change the typecasting of arguments, instead of using a different language all together.
  2. 2. Which frameworks and languages should I focus on once I progress farther with vanilla JS.
